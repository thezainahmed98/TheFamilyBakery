About Us

Found in 1950 by World War II veteran, Joseph Anderson and his loving wife Margaret Anderson. Initially the name was registered as “The Anderson’s Bakery” but overtime, it became locally known as “The Family Bakery” for being the only family-run bakery in the area. We have been proudly serving the Chicagoland area for more than 60 years and will continue to do so!
